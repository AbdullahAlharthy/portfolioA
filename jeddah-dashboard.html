District Tier Effect
Tier 3 (Luxury) districts command 2.4× higher median rents vs Tier 0 (Economic). The district encoding feature alone explains 12.92% of model variance (with embeddings).
NLP Signal
Arabic & English listing descriptions — processed via Sentence Transformers — contribute 26.9% of total model importance, identifying sea-view and premium keywords.
Amenity Score
The engineered amenity_score feature (0–10, KNN-imputed for Aqar data) is the second most important feature at 7.1% importance in CatBoost (after embeddings).
Spatial Features
Distance to CBD and corniche, computed via Haversine formula, combine with district tier to create interaction features (cbd_x_tier, sea_x_premium) that boost predictive power.
