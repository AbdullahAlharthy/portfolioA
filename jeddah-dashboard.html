⚠ Model Limitations
Listed vs Actual Price
Training data reflects asking prices, not final negotiated rents. Actual rents may differ.
Missing Qualitative Features
Unit condition, floor quality, view direction, and building reputation are not captured in the data.
District-Level Granularity
Location is encoded at the district level. Street-level or block-level variation is not modeled.
Static Snapshot
The model reflects market conditions at data collection time. Seasonal or economic shifts are not accounted for.
